Digital Ethics

Using Technology with Integrity

As responsible digital citizens, we should use technology with honesty and respect. What we do online shows who we are as a person. Using the internet in a good way helps create a safe and positive space for everyone.

Online kindness is very important. We should think before we post, avoid hurtful words, and respect other people’s opinions. Being kind online is the same as being kind in real life.

We must also be honest in our digital work. Avoid plagiarism, respect other people’s privacy, and give credit when using others’ ideas. By doing these, we show good values and true Christian behavior in the digital world.
