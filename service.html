Serving with Purpose

"Light of the World" in Action

Community service lets me show God’s love by helping others. I join church activities and school projects to do good and make a difference. Even small acts of kindness can make someone’s day better. I also take part in outreach programs to help people who need it. I volunteer at charity events, join clean-up drives, and encourage others to do good too. Every little effort counts. Through these activities, I want to be a light in my community. By helping and inspiring others, I hope to make my community a happier and kinder place.
