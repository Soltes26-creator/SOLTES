Personal Reflection

"Una ang Ginoo"

As I grow older, I am learning to become more responsible and closer to God. Every day, I remind myself that everything I do should start with Him. “Una ang Ginoo” helps me make better choices in my life. In a world full of distractions, I try to stay focused on what is right. I choose to be kind, honest, and purposeful. I’m not perfect, but I continue to to learn and improve. With God as my guide, I know I can grow and become a better person.
